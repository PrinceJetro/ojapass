The Economic Heart of OjaPass

The OjaScore is a dynamic, AI-driven trust metric that replaces traditional credit scores, capturing the reality of Nigeria's informal economy.

1. How the Score is Calculated

trending_up

Financial Velocity

Every sale logged via Mamatally and every payment received through Squad adds "Growth Points." It proves your business is active and generating cash flow.

savings

Savings Discipline (The Heavyweight)

Consistent, on-time Ajo contributions are our strongest signal of reliability. On-time payments boost you rapidly; missing one is the fastest way to drop your score.

handshake

Marketplace Reputation

Completing gigs and getting high ratings provides "Trust Points." For employers, maintaining a high "Fill Rate" for postings boosts your standing.
